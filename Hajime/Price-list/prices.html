MiyaGi and the Endgame initially performed separately, but in 2013 the joint album of Endgame, Namo Minigana and MiyaGi, entitled “EnaMi”, was released. Also, during the stay of both artists in Dope Records, their joint songs were released: Ma Ja (feat. SH Kera), Fur-oak (feat. SH Kera, Namo Minigan), Nowhere to go (feat. Acab), Slander (feat. Acab , Namo Minigan). Azamat decided to create a duet with Soslan in 2015. The duo’s first work was the single “Sanavabich”, which was released with the clip in 2015. The duet in 2015 took part in the song of Eljey "Music". And there was also a joint song, “Releases,” with Anar Zeynalov, a member of the Caspian Cargo group.
